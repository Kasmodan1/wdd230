Explore Carlsbad, Southern California

Carlsbad is a charming coastal city located in Southern California, known for its stunning beaches, beautiful flower fields, and adventurous outdoor activities. It's a paradise for surfers with spots like Tamarack Surf Beach and South Carlsbad State Beach offering consistent waves. The Carlsbad Flower Fields, open to the public from March through early May, showcase a spectacular display of ranunculus flowers spread across 50 acres. The city is also home to LEGOLAND California, a family-friendly theme park with over 60 rides, shows, and attractions. For nature enthusiasts, the Agua Hedionda Lagoon offers boating, fishing, and hiking. Furthermore, Carlsbad Village, the downtown district, offers unique shopping experiences and delicious dining options. Discover Carlsbad, and experience the allure of Southern California's laid-back beach culture!
