Target Audience

The Grants Pass Chamber of Commerce website is designed to serve the needs of small business owners and entrepreneurs in the Grants Pass community. Our goal is to provide resources and support that promote economic growth and development, enhance the overall quality of life, and foster community partnerships.

Personas

  • Jenna, is an entrepreneur: Jenna is a 34-year-old graphic designer, who recently launched her own branding and design LLC. She's passionate about helping local businesses create memorable brands and marketing campaigns, but she's looking for ways to connect with other entrepreneurs and expand her client base.
  • Mike, is an established business owner: Mike is a 50-year-old owner of a construction company, that has been serving the Grants Pass community for over 20 years. He's looking for ways to modernize his business and stay competitive in an ever changing market, but also wants to give back to the community.
